2013

Sac De Jour

The duffel bag, newly introduced in January 2013 at Saint Laurent, is meaningful in that it is a "Permanent" collection that Saint Laurent's creative director was named Eddie Slyman and was first introduced after re-routed to Saint Laurent. The duffel bag features a simple design and gold embossed logo on the front of the bag. It will be presented in five sizes, named Duffle 3, Duffle6, Duffle 12, Duffle24, and Duffle48, meaning the number after the bag name means the time you can spend with the bag. For example, Duple 24 is the right size for a one-day trip, while Duple 48 is the right size for a two-day trip.
